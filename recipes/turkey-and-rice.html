Ground Turkey and Rice


Description

This recipie is a simple meal to throw together in a hurry. All it takes is some browned ground turkey with whatever seasoning you like, and then cooked rice and some cheese for garnish.


Ingredients (makes 4 servings)

  • 1 pound ground turkey
  • 2 cups white rice
  • Seasoning, to taste (I usually just use seasoned salt)
  • 1 cup shredded cheese
  • Hot sauce, optional, to taste

Steps

  1. Using a rice cooker, cook the rice according to the directions for the cooker.
  2. While the rice is cooking, brown the turkey and add seasonings to taste.
  3. Once the rice is cooked, spoon equally into bowls.
  4. Once the meat is browned, add to the bowls on top of the cooked rice.
  5. Add shredded cheese on top of the meat.
  6. Add hot sauce if desired and enjoy!
